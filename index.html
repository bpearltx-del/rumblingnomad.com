Trip Highlights
Devil's Highway (US-491) — Rides through Blanding → Bluff → Cortez into Durango on Day 4. Remote red canyon walls, almost no traffic, high desert at its finest.
Million Dollar Highway (US-550) — Rally Day 1. Ride north to Ouray first, then southbound back through Silverton. Mountain wall on your left all the way down Red Mountain Pass (11,018 ft).
Monument Valley — First night of the return. Dedicated overnight so you catch golden hour on the Mittens and ride the 17-mile valley drive.
Zion NP — UT-9 through the canyon on the return. The canyon walls from the saddle are jaw-dropping. $35 entry covered by America the Beautiful pass.
Book NOW — Moab Best Western, Best Western View of Lake Powell (Page), and Goulding's Lodge (Monument Valley) all fill up in June. Don't wait.
Zion Timed Entry — Check recreation.gov for advance permits. Required in peak season.
Notify your bank — Flag WA, ID, UT, CO, AZ, NV or your card may get blocked at a remote fuel pump in Navajo Nation with no cell service.
